## Summary
Strengthen hamstrings (intense eccentric) and improve functional strengthening.

## Execution
- Slow, controlled eccentric movement
- body aligned knees-shoulders
- hands on ground if necessary
- Execution: body aligned knees-shoulders

## Breathing
Regular (no prolonged apnea).

## Tips
- Partial Nordic + hand assistance.
- Nordic complete slow eccentric (3-5 s).
- 2-4 x 3-6 reps Rest 90-120 s.

## Dosage
2-4 x 3-6 reps Rest 90-120 s.

## Safety
Adapted amplitude: no joint pain. Control: active sheathing, clean movement, regular breathing.
