## Summary
Stretch the hamstrings with a straight-leg forward fold.

## Execution
- Seated on the floor, one leg extended, the other tucked in
- Flat back, lean forward from the hips toward the extended foot
- Hands reaching toward foot or ankle without rounding the back
- Hold the position without bouncing

## Breathing
Slow and deep. Release with each exhale.

## Tips
- Easier: use a strap around the foot, slightly bend the knee.
- Harder: both legs extended, deeper fold.

## Dosage
30-45 s per side × 2-3 sets. Rest 15-30 s.

## Safety
Flat back mandatory: do not round the spine. Gentle hinge from the hips. No pain behind the knee.
