## Summary
Leg raise on arm supports targeting abs and hip flexors. Among the most effective exercises for the rectus abdominis.

## Execution
- Back against the pad, forearms on the arm rests
- Grip the handles firmly
- Let legs hang vertically
- Engage abs before starting the movement
- Raise knees or straight legs toward the chest
- Lift until thighs are parallel to the floor or higher
- Lower slowly without swinging

## Breathing
Exhale while raising legs, inhale while lowering.

## Tips
- Beginner: bent-knee raises
- Intermediate: straight legs to horizontal
- Advanced: add ankle weights or static L-sit hold
- Lateral rotation to target obliques

## Dosage
3 x 10-15 reps bent knees (beginner).
3-4 x 8-12 reps straight legs (intermediate).

## Safety
Do NOT swing legs for momentum. Slow, controlled movement. Engage core before each rep. Keep back against the pad.
