## Summary
Stretch the chest and biceps using a wall for support.

## Execution
- Arm at shoulder height, hand and forearm against the wall
- Slowly rotate the torso away from the arm
- Shoulder down, do not arch the back
- Hold without forcing

## Breathing
Slow and deep. Increase rotation with each exhale.

## Tips
- Easier: arm lower on the wall, moderate rotation.
- Harder: arm higher to target the upper fibers.

## Dosage
30-45 s per side × 2-3 sets. Rest 15-30 s.

## Safety
Shoulder down at all times. Do not arch the back. Progressive rotation, no shoulder pain.
