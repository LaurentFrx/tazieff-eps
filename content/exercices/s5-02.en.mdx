## Summary
Simplified burpee without push-up, focused on cardio endurance and coordination.

## Execution
- From standing, squat down and place hands on the floor
- Kick feet back into a plank position (no push-up)
- Jump feet back toward hands
- Stand up and jump vertically with arms overhead

## Breathing
Exhale when kicking feet back and on the jump. Inhale when returning feet and standing up.

## Tips
- Easier: step back one foot at a time instead of jumping.
- Harder: increase tempo, progress toward full burpees (s5-01).
- Suggested dosage: 3-5 x 6-15 reps, rest 60-90 s.

## Dosage
3-5 x 6-15 reps. Rest 60-90 s.

## Safety
Active core bracing in plank position. Controlled transitions without rushing. Adjust pace to fatigue level.
