## Summary
Quad isolation exercise on a machine. Ideal for warm-up or as a finisher after compound exercises.

## Execution
- Back flat against the pad
- Roller positioned just above the ankles (front of the shin)
- Knees aligned with the machine's pivot point
- Extend legs to near-full extension
- Squeeze the quads at the top (1-2 s)
- Bend knees in a controlled manner

## Breathing
Exhale while extending, inhale while bending.

## Tips
- Excellent as warm-up/activation before squats or leg press
- Favor longer sets with moderate loads
- Slow eccentric tempo (3 s) to maximize muscle work

## Dosage
3-4 x 12-15 reps moderate loads (hypertrophy).
2 x 15-20 reps light load (warm-up/activation).

## Safety
Do not lock knees at full extension. Moderate loads recommended (high shear force on the knee). Slow, controlled movement. Avoid if you have knee issues.
