## Summary
Strengthen pectoral/triceps strength and shoulder stability.

## Execution
- Complete trunk rotation with
- arms form a perfect vertical line
- balance and control
- Execution: arms form perfect vertical line

## Breathing
Regular (no prolonged apnea).

## Tips
- Reduce amplitude and/or use support (bench, TRX, hands).
- Add a load, increase tempo or difficulty (instability).
- 3-5 x 6-15 reps Rest 60-90 s.

## Dosage
3-5 x 6-15 reps Rest 60-90 s.

## Safety
Adapted amplitude: no joint pain. Control: active sheathing, clean movement, regular breathing.
