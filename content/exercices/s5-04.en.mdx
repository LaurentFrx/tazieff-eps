## Summary
Compound movement combining a squat and overhead press for explosive full-body work.

## Execution
- Dumbbells in rack position (on shoulders), feet shoulder-width apart
- Lower into a full squat (thighs parallel to the floor)
- Drive up explosively and press dumbbells directly overhead
- Lower dumbbells back to shoulders and flow into the next squat

## Breathing
Inhale during the squat descent. Exhale on the drive up and press.

## Tips
- Easier: lighter weight or a stick, shallower squat.
- Harder: increase load, chain reps without pause.
- Suggested dosage: 3-5 x 8-15 reps, rest 60-90 s.

## Dosage
3-5 x 8-15 reps. Rest 60-90 s.

## Safety
Drive comes from the legs, not the arms. Fluid continuous movement between squat and press. Active core bracing, straight back.
