## Summary
Guided lower body push movement. A safe alternative to squats for developing quads and glutes.

## Execution
- Back and head flat against the pad
- Feet on the platform, shoulder-width apart, toes slightly outward
- Release the safety mechanism
- Bend knees to bring the platform toward you (controlled descent)
- Lower until knees reach approximately 90°
- Push through heels and forefoot
- NEVER lock the knees at the top

## Breathing
Inhale while lowering, exhale while pushing.

## Tips
- Feet high: emphasizes glutes and hamstrings
- Feet low: emphasizes quads (watch the knees)
- Feet wide: emphasizes adductors

## Dosage
3-5 x 10-20 reps Rest 60-90 s (hypertrophy).
3-6 x 5-10 reps heavy load Rest 2-3 min (strength).

## Safety
NEVER lock knees in full extension. Do not lift lower back off the pad. Knees tracking over toes. Always use the safety catches.
