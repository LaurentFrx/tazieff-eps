## Summary
Strengthen Grand droit complet and improve functional strengthening.

## Execution
- Balance on the glutes
- touch the toes at the top
- controlled descent without completely resting the back
- Execution: touching the toes at the top
- 4 reps / side x 2-4
- Technical priority.

## Breathing
Regular (no prolonged apnea).

## Tips
- Reduce amplitude and/or use support (bench, TRX, hands).
- Add a load, increase tempo or difficulty (instability).
- Content to be completed

## Dosage
Content to complete

## Safety
Adapted amplitude: no joint pain. Control: active sheathing, clean movement, regular breathing.
