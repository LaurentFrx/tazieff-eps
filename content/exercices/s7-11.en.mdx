## Summary
The barbell squat is the king of lower body exercises. The squat rack provides a safe environment with safety bars.

## Execution
- Set J-hooks just below shoulder height and safety bars below the bottom squat position
- Bar on upper traps, overhand wide grip
- Unrack and step back, feet shoulder-width or wider
- Deep breath, brace the core (intra-abdominal pressure)
- Lower by bending hips and knees simultaneously, as if sitting down
- Thighs at least parallel to the floor, knees tracking over toes
- Drive through heels to stand back up

## Breathing
Deep breath before descent, brace, exhale while standing up.

## Tips
- Start with an empty bar to master technique
- Toes turned outward (15-30°)
- A spotter is recommended for heavy loads

## Dosage
3-5 x 3-6 reps Rest 3-5 min (strength).
3-4 x 8-12 reps Rest 2 min (hypertrophy).

## Safety
ALWAYS set the safety bars. Spotter recommended for heavy loads. Never train alone without safety catches. Bar collars mandatory. On failure: lean forward and let the bar rest on the safety bars.
