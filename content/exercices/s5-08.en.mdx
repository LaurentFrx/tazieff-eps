## Summary
Explosive exercise combining a squat and a medicine ball throw against a wall.

## Execution
- Medicine ball held against the chest, facing a wall (about 1 m away)
- Lower into a deep squat
- Explode upward and throw the ball at a high target on the wall
- Catch the ball on the return, absorb and flow into the next squat

## Breathing
Inhale during the squat descent. Exhale forcefully on the throw.

## Tips
- Easier: lighter ball, lower target, shallower squat.
- Harder: heavier ball, higher target, sustained pace.
- Suggested dosage: 3-5 x 8-15 reps, rest 60-90 s.

## Dosage
3-5 x 8-15 reps. Rest 60-90 s.

## Safety
Throw power comes from the legs, not the arms. Cushioned ball catch. Straight back during the squat. Appropriate distance from wall.
