## Summary
Stretch the deltoid and rotator cuff by pulling the arm across the chest.

## Execution
- Arm extended across the chest at shoulder height
- With the opposite hand, gently pull the arm toward you
- Shoulder down, do not shrug the stretched shoulder
- Hold without forcing

## Breathing
Slow and steady. Release the shoulder with each exhale.

## Tips
- Easier: light pressure, arm slightly bent.
- Harder: slightly rotate the torso away from the stretched side.

## Dosage
30-45 s per side × 2-3 sets. Rest 15-30 s.

## Safety
Shoulder down at all times. Gentle pressure with the opposite hand. No shoulder pain.
