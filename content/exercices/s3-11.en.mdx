## Summary
Strengthen upper pectorals (clavicular portion) and improve functional strengthening.

## Execution
- Optimum angle 30-45
- not too vertical (would put too much strain on shoulders)
- full amplitude
- Execution: not too vertical (would strain shoulders too much)

## Breathing
Regular (no prolonged apnea).

## Tips
- Reduce amplitude and/or use support (bench, TRX, hands).
- Add a load, increase tempo or difficulty (instability).
- 3-5 x 8-15 reps Rest 60-90 s.

## Dosage
3-5 x 8-15 reps Rest 60-90 s.

## Safety
Adapted amplitude: no joint pain. Control: active sheathing, clean movement, regular breathing.
