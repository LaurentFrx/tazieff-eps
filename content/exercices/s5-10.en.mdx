## Summary
Develop lower body power and vertical leap through box jumps.

## Execution
- Face the box, feet shoulder-width apart
- Swing arms back then jump extending hips and knees
- Land with full feet on the box, knees bent to absorb
- Stand fully at the top, then step down with control

## Breathing
Inhale before the jump. Exhale at the moment of takeoff.

## Tips
- Easier: lower box height, step-ups instead of jumps.
- Harder: higher box, chain without pause, add weight.
- Suggested dosage: 3-6 x 4-8 jumps, rest 60-90 s.

## Dosage
3-6 x 4-8 jumps. Rest 60-90 s. Quality over quantity.

## Safety
Land with full feet on the box (not on the edge). Knees aligned with feet on landing. Step down with control (do not jump down). Stable non-slip surface.
