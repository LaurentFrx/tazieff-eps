## Summary
Stretch the lats and lower back in a curled-up position on the floor.

## Execution
- Lying on back, bring knees toward chest
- Wrap arms around the knees
- Head relaxed, shoulders released
- Hold the position, breathing calmly

## Breathing
Slow and deep. Release shoulders and back with each exhale.

## Tips
- Easier: knees slightly apart for more comfort.
- Harder: gentle rocking motions forward and back to massage the lower back.

## Dosage
30-45 s × 2-3 sets. Rest 15-30 s.

## Safety
Soft surface (mat). Do not pull on the neck. Gentle controlled movement.
