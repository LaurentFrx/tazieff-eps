## Summary
Strengthen the abdominal muscles and improve core stability.

## Execution
- Pelvis in neutral position
- gaze towards the ground
- continuous breathing
- Execution: look down

## Breathing
Regular (no prolonged apnea).

## Tips

- Longer lever, instability (Swiss ball) or duration +.
- 3-5 x 20-60 s Rest 30-60 s Posture quality.

## Dosage
