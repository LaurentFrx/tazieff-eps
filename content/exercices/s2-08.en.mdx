## Summary
Supine bicycle combining trunk flexion and rotation.

## Execution
- Maximum trunk rotation
- opposite leg straight, low to the ground
- sustained rhythm
- Execution: opposite leg straight, low to the ground

## Breathing
Regular (no prolonged apnea).

## Tips
- Reduce amplitude and/or use support (bench, TRX, hands).
- Add a load, increase tempo or difficulty (instability).

## Dosage
3-5 x 10-20 reps Rest 45-75 s Control.

## Safety
Adapted amplitude: no joint pain. Control: active sheathing, clean movement, regular breathing.
