## Summary
Strengthen core bracing, grip and trunk stability through loaded carries.

## Execution
- Hold a dumbbell in each hand, arms at sides
- Shoulders back and down, chest open
- Walk with regular controlled steps for the planned distance
- Active core bracing, upright posture throughout

## Breathing
Regular continuous breathing. Do not hold breath despite the load.

## Tips
- Easier: lighter weight, shorter distance (10-15 m).
- Harder: increase weight or distance, single-arm carry (one hand only).
- Suggested dosage: 3-5 x 20-40 m, rest 60-90 s.

## Dosage
3-5 x 20-40 m. Rest 60-90 s.

## Safety
Upright posture mandatory: do not lean to one side. Firm grip on dumbbells. Controlled steps, avoid running.
