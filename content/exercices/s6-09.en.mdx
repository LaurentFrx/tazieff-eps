## Summary
Stretch the calves leaning against a wall with the back leg straight.

## Execution
- Hands on the wall, front leg bent, back leg straight behind
- Back heel on the floor, back knee straight
- Push hips toward the wall to increase the stretch
- Hold without bouncing

## Breathing
Slow and steady. Release the calf with each exhale.

## Tips
- Easier: shorter distance from the wall.
- Harder: slightly bend the back knee to target the soleus.

## Dosage
30-45 s per side × 2-3 sets. Rest 15-30 s.

## Safety
Heel on the floor at all times. No bouncing. No Achilles tendon pain.
