## Summary
Strengthen the posterior chain (hamstrings, glutes, erectors) through a controlled hip hinge.

## Execution
- Standing, feet hip-width apart, dumbbells held in front of thighs
- Hinge forward pushing hips back
- Dumbbells travel down along the legs, knees slightly bent
- Rise by squeezing the glutes, flat back from start to finish

## Breathing
Inhale during the descent. Exhale on the way up.

## Tips
- Easier: reduced range of motion, lighter weight.
- Harder: increase load, slow the eccentric (3-4 s descent).
- Suggested dosage: 3-5 x 8-12 reps, rest 60-90 s.

## Dosage
3-5 x 8-12 reps. Rest 60-90 s.

## Safety
Flat back mandatory: hinge at the hips, never at the back. Dumbbells stay close to the legs. Knees slightly bent but not in a squat.
