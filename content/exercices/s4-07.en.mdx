## Summary
Dumbbell Romanian deadlift for the posterior chain.

## Execution
- Flat back IMPERATIVE
- dumbbells shave legs
- hip flexion (not back flexion)
- Execution: dumbbells shave legs

## Breathing
Regular (no prolonged apnea).

## Tips
- Reduce amplitude and/or use support (bench, TRX, hands).
- Add a load, increase tempo or difficulty (instability).

## Dosage
3-5 x 6-12 reps Rest 60-120 s.

## Safety
Neutral back: hip hinge, avoid rounding. Adapted amplitude: no joint pain. Control: active sheathing,
