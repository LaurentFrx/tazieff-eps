## Summary
Strengthen Gastrocnemius and improve functional strengthening.

## Execution
- Full amplitude (max stretching and contraction)
- tempo control 2-1-2
- unilateral option for increased intensity
- Execution: tempo control 2-1-2

## Breathing
Regular (no prolonged apnea).

## Tips
- Reduce amplitude and/or use support (bench, TRX, hands).
- Add a load, increase tempo or difficulty (instability).
- 3-5 x 12-20 reps Tempo control (2-1-2).

## Dosage
3-5 x 12-20 reps Tempo control (2-1-2).

## Safety
Adapted amplitude: no joint pain. Control: active sheathing, clean movement, regular breathing.
