## Summary
Strengthen pectoral/triceps strength and shoulder stability.

## Execution
- Elbows down at 90
- chest slightly bent (focus on pectorals) or straight (focus on
- triceps)
- controlled amplitude
- Execution: chest slightly bent (pectoral focus) or straight (triceps focus)

## Tips
- Reduce amplitude and/or use support (bench, TRX, hands).
- Add a load, increase tempo or difficulty (instability).
- 3-5 x 8-15 reps Rest 60-90 s.

## Dosage
3-5 x 8-15 reps Rest 60-90 s.

## Safety
Low shoulders: avoid collapsing at the bottom of the movement. Adapted amplitude: no joint pain.
