## Summary
Guided vertical push targeting deltoids and triceps. The guided movement allows focus on muscle contraction.

## Execution
- Adjust seat height so handles are at shoulder level
- Back flat against the pad, feet flat on the floor
- Grip the handles, elbows at 90°
- Push handles upward to near-full arm extension
- Do not lock elbows at the top
- Lower in a controlled manner

## Breathing
Exhale while pushing up, inhale while lowering.

## Tips
- Keep shoulders down (do not shrug)
- Good alternative to the overhead press for beginners
- Some machines allow single-arm work

## Dosage
3-4 x 8-12 reps Rest 60-90 s (hypertrophy).
2-3 x 12-15 reps (endurance).

## Safety
Do not arch the back excessively. Do not lock elbows. Adjust load for full range of motion.
