## Summary
Cable biceps curl providing constant tension throughout the full range of motion, unlike dumbbells.

## Execution
- Attach the straight bar or rope to the low pulley
- Face the machine, feet shoulder-width apart
- Grip the bar underhand, arms at your sides
- Elbows pinned to the body, locked in position
- Curl the bar up toward the shoulders
- Squeeze the biceps at the top (1 s)
- Lower slowly in a controlled manner

## Breathing
Exhale while curling up, inhale while lowering.

## Tips
- Cable advantage: constant tension vs dumbbells (variable tension depending on angle)
- Hammer grip with rope: emphasizes brachialis and forearms
- Keep elbows stationary throughout

## Dosage
3-4 x 10-12 reps Rest 60-90 s (hypertrophy).
2-3 x 15-20 reps (endurance).

## Safety
Do not swing the torso. Keep elbows fixed. Use a load that allows controlled movement.
