## Summary
Complete functional exercise transitioning from the floor to standing with a load overhead.

## Execution
- Lying on back, kettlebell held with arm extended toward the ceiling
- Rise through 7 steps: floor, elbow, hand, bridge, knee, lunge, standing
- Reverse each step with control to return to the floor
- Eyes stay fixed on the kettlebell throughout the movement

## Breathing
Continuous regular breathing. Exhale during effort phases (rising), inhale during transitions.

## Tips
- Easier: no weight or balance a shoe on the fist.
- Harder: progressively increase kettlebell weight.
- Suggested dosage: 2-4 x 3-5 reps per side, rest 60-120 s.

## Dosage
2-4 x 3-5 reps per side. Rest 60-120 s. Absolute technique priority.

## Safety
Master each step before adding load. Shoulder locked, arm extended at all times. Never take eyes off the kettlebell.
