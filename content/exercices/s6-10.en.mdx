## Summary
Full back and shoulder stretch in a kneeling forward fold.

## Execution
- Kneeling, sit hips back toward heels
- Lean torso forward, forehead toward the floor
- Arms extended in front or alongside the body
- Fully release shoulders and back

## Breathing
Slow and deep. Let the back lengthen with each exhale.

## Tips
- Easier: spread knees apart for more belly room.
- Harder: reach arms as far forward as possible.

## Dosage
45-60 s × 2-3 sets. Rest 15-30 s.

## Safety
Soft surface (mat). No knee pain (use a cushion if needed). Gentle movement.
