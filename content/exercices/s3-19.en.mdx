## Summary
Strengthen the triceps brachii and improve functional strengthening.

## Execution
- Stationary elbow parallel to trunk
- full extension with voluntary contraction
- one arm at a time
- Execution: full extension with voluntary contraction

## Breathing
Regular (no prolonged apnea).

## Tips
- Reduce amplitude and/or use support (bench, TRX, hands).
- Add a load, increase tempo or difficulty (instability).
- 3-5 x 8-15 reps Rest 60-90 s.

## Dosage
3-5 x 8-15 reps Rest 60-90 s.

## Safety
Adapted amplitude: no joint pain. Control: active sheathing, clean movement, regular breathing.
