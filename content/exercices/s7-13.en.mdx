## Summary
Fundamental horizontal push movement targeting chest, front delts, and triceps. The king of upper body exercises.

## Execution
- Lie on the bench, eyes under the bar, feet flat on the floor
- Retract shoulder blades, shoulders back and down
- Overhand grip slightly wider than shoulder width
- Unrack the bar (with spotter if using significant load)
- Lower the bar toward mid-chest in a controlled manner
- Touch the chest lightly without bouncing
- Push upward to full arm extension

## Breathing
Inhale while lowering, exhale while pushing.

## Tips
- Clear communication with spotter (liftoff signal and help signal)
- Neutral wrists, do not bend backward
- Natural arch in the lower back (not excessive)

## Dosage
3-5 x 3-6 reps Rest 3-5 min (strength).
3-4 x 8-12 reps Rest 2 min (hypertrophy).

## Safety
ALWAYS use a spotter for significant loads. NSCA spotting technique: spotter standing behind, alternating grip. Bar collars mandatory. Never lift feet off the floor or hips off the bench. Do not bounce the bar off the chest.
