## Summary
Develop posterior chain power through an explosive kettlebell swing.

## Execution
- Feet shoulder-width apart, kettlebell on the floor in front
- Grip the kettlebell with both hands, hinge hips back
- Drive the kettlebell forward with an explosive hip extension
- Arms straight, kettlebell rises to shoulder height then swings back between the legs

## Breathing
Exhale forcefully on hip extension. Inhale as the kettlebell swings back between the legs.

## Tips
- Easier: lighter kettlebell (8 kg), reduced range of motion.
- Harder: heavier kettlebell, chain into EMOM or circuit.
- Suggested dosage: 4-6 x 10-20 reps, rest 45-75 s.

## Dosage
4-6 x 10-20 reps. Rest 45-75 s. Explosive movement.

## Safety
Movement comes from the hips, not the arms. Flat back at all times, never rounded. Eyes forward. Firm grip on the kettlebell.
