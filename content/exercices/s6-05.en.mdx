## Summary
Stretch the quadriceps by pulling the heel toward the glute while standing.

## Execution
- Stand on one leg, grab the opposite foot behind you
- Knees together, neutral pelvis (do not arch)
- Gently pull the heel toward the glute
- Hold without tilting the pelvis

## Breathing
Slow and steady. Release with each exhale.

## Tips
- Easier: hold a wall or partner for balance.
- Harder: push the knee backward to deepen the stretch.

## Dosage
30-45 s per side × 2-3 sets. Rest 15-30 s.

## Safety
Neutral pelvis: do not arch the back. Knees together. No knee pain.
