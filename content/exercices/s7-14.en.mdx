## Summary
Fundamental pull from the floor engaging the entire posterior chain. The most complete exercise in strength training.

## Execution
- Bar on the floor over mid-foot, feet hip-width apart
- Grip the bar overhand, hands just outside the knees
- Chest out, flat back, shoulders over or slightly in front of the bar
- Deep breath, brace the core
- Drive the floor away while pulling the bar along the shins
- Extend hips and knees simultaneously (bar stays close to the body)
- Stand fully upright, shoulders back, hips locked
- Lower by reversing the movement, flat back

## Breathing
Deep breath before the pull, brace, exhale at the top.

## Tips
- Start with very light loads for technique
- Bar always in contact with the body (shins, thighs)
- Mixed grip for heavy loads (watch for rotational imbalance)
- Never train to failure (injury risk when technique breaks down)

## Dosage
3-5 x 3-5 reps Rest 3-5 min (strength).
3-4 x 6-8 reps Rest 2-3 min (hypertrophy).

## Safety
The deadlift CANNOT BE SPOTTED (NSCA). On failure: simply drop the bar. NEVER round the back. Bar collars mandatory. Stable, non-slip surface.
