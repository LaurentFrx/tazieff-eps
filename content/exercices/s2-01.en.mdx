## Summary
Strengthen the abdominal muscles and improve functional strengthening. Rhythmic breathing. Controlled or explosive tempo depending on objective.

## Execution
- Hips stable without swaying

## Tips
- Reduce amplitude and/or use support (bench, TRX, hands).
- Add a load, increase tempo or difficulty (instability).
- 3-6 x 20-40 s Rest 20-40 s Trunk stable.

## Dosage
3-6 x 20-40 s Rest 20-40 s Core stable.

## Safety
Adapted amplitude: no joint pain. Control: active sheathing, clean movement, regular breathing.
