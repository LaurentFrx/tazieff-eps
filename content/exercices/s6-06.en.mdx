## Summary
Stretch the adductors seated with soles of feet together.

## Execution
- Seated on the floor, soles of feet together, knees apart
- Straight back, hands on ankles or feet
- Let the knees drop toward the floor by gravity
- Hold without pushing the knees down

## Breathing
Calm and steady. Release the hips with each exhale.

## Tips
- Easier: sit on a cushion to elevate the pelvis.
- Harder: lean the torso forward with a flat back.

## Dosage
30-45 s × 2-3 sets. Rest 15-30 s.

## Safety
Do not force the knees down. Let gravity do the work. No groin pain.
