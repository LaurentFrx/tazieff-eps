## Summary
Strengthen the pull (back/biceps) and improve posture.

## Execution
- Complete descent with arms outstretched
- no kipping
- chest towards bar
- Execution: no kipping
- 6 x 3-10 reps
- Rest 90 sec.
- Technique.

## Breathing
Regular (no prolonged apnea).

## Tips
- Reduce amplitude and/or use support (bench, TRX, hands).
- Add a load, increase tempo or difficulty (instability).
- Content to be completed

## Dosage
Content to complete

## Safety
Adapted amplitude: no joint pain. Control: active sheathing, clean movement, regular breathing.
