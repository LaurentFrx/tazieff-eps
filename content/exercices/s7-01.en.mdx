## Summary
Guided vertical pull targeting the lats, traps, and rhomboids. Great exercise for building back width.

## Execution
- Sit facing the machine, thighs secured under the pads
- Grip the bar overhand, wider than shoulder width
- Chest out, retract shoulder blades down and back
- Pull the bar toward the upper chest by driving elbows down
- Pause with shoulder blades squeezed together
- Return the bar in a controlled manner to full arm extension

## Breathing
Exhale while pulling the bar down, inhale while returning.

## Tips
- Pull with elbows, not hands (better back recruitment)
- Underhand grip: emphasizes biceps
- Neutral grip: reduces shoulder stress
- Close grip: targets lower lats

## Dosage
3-4 x 8-12 reps Rest 60-90 s (hypertrophy).
2-3 x 15-20 reps Rest 30-60 s (endurance).
4-5 x 3-6 reps Rest 2-3 min Explosive concentric (power).

## Safety
Never pull behind the neck. Do not lean back excessively. No body momentum.
