## Summary
Triceps isolation exercise on a cable machine. The rope allows spreading the wrists at the bottom to maximize contraction.

## Execution
- Attach the rope to the high pulley
- Face the machine, feet hip-width apart, knees slightly bent
- Grip both ends of the rope, elbows pinned to the body
- Push the rope down to full elbow extension
- At the bottom, spread wrists slightly outward
- Return in a controlled manner to 90°

## Breathing
Exhale while pushing down, inhale while returning.

## Tips
- Keep elbows fixed, pinned to the body throughout
- With a straight or V-bar: allows heavier loads
- Single-arm extension: corrects imbalances

## Dosage
3-4 x 10-15 reps Rest 60 s (hypertrophy).
2-3 x 15-20 reps (finisher).

## Safety
Do not let elbows flare out. Keep back straight, do not lean forward. Controlled movement, no momentum.
