## Summary
Chest isolation exercise on a guided machine. The hugging motion effectively targets the pectoralis major.

## Execution
- Adjust the seat so elbows are at chest level
- Back flat against the pad, feet flat on the floor
- Forearms on the pads, elbows bent at 90°
- Bring arms together in front of the chest (hugging motion)
- Squeeze the chest at the point of maximum contraction (1 s)
- Open arms in a controlled manner without going past shoulder alignment

## Breathing
Exhale while bringing arms together, inhale while opening.

## Tips
- Keep shoulder blades retracted, chest out
- Great as a pre-fatigue exercise before bench press
- Slow eccentric tempo (3 s return) for beginners

## Dosage
3-4 x 10-15 reps Rest 60-90 s (hypertrophy).
2-3 x 15-20 reps (finisher/pre-fatigue).

## Safety
Do not open arms too far back. Slow, controlled movement. Do not use a load that rounds the shoulders forward.
