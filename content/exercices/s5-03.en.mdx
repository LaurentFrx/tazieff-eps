## Summary
Simplified burpee variant without push-up or jump, focused on legs and core.

## Execution
- From standing, squat down and place hands on the floor
- Kick feet back into a plank position
- Immediately jump feet back toward hands
- Stand up (no jump)

## Breathing
Exhale when kicking feet back, inhale on the return. Regular and rhythmic breathing.

## Tips
- Easier: step back one foot at a time.
- Harder: add a vertical jump, then a push-up to progress toward full burpees.
- Suggested dosage: 3-5 x 8-15 reps, rest 60-90 s.

## Dosage
3-5 x 8-15 reps. Rest 60-90 s.

## Safety
Active core bracing in plank position. Wrists placed under shoulders. Pace adapted to level.
