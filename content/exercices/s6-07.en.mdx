## Summary
Stretch the glutes and piriformis by crossing an ankle over the opposite knee.

## Execution
- Lying on back, cross one ankle over the opposite knee
- Gently pull the support leg toward the chest
- Head on the floor, shoulders relaxed
- Hold the position, breathing calmly

## Breathing
Slow and deep. Release the hip with each exhale.

## Tips
- Easier: keep the support foot on the floor instead of pulling the leg.
- Harder: pull the leg closer to the chest.

## Dosage
30-45 s per side × 2-3 sets. Rest 15-30 s.

## Safety
Do not lift the head off the floor. Gentle progressive pressure. No knee pain.
