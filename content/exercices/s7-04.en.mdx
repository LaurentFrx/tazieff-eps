## Summary
Biceps isolation exercise on an inclined preacher bench. The support eliminates cheating and targets the short head of the biceps.

## Execution
- Adjust the seat so armpits rest on the upper edge of the pad
- Arms on the pad, elbows slightly bent (never locked)
- Grip the EZ bar with an underhand grip
- Curl the bar up toward the shoulders
- Squeeze the biceps at the top (1 s)
- Lower in a controlled manner, arms staying on the pad

## Breathing
Exhale while curling up, inhale while lowering.

## Tips
- Use dumbbells (single arm) to correct imbalances
- Hammer grip: emphasizes the brachialis and brachioradialis
- Progress load very gradually

## Dosage
3-4 x 8-12 reps Rest 60-90 s (hypertrophy).
2-3 x 15-20 reps light load (endurance).

## Safety
Never fully extend elbows at the bottom (tendon risk). Do not lift arms off the pad. Use a load that allows proper technique.
