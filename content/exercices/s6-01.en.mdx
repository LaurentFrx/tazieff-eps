## Summary
Stretch the psoas and quadriceps in a low lunge position.

## Execution
- Back knee on the floor, front foot flat ahead
- Push the hips forward with a posterior pelvic tilt
- Back straight, gaze forward
- Hold the position without bouncing

## Breathing
Slow and deep. Release further with each exhale.

## Tips
- Easier: place a cushion under the back knee.
- Harder: raise the arm on the stretched side to amplify the stretch.

## Dosage
30-45 s per side × 2-3 sets. Rest 15-30 s.

## Safety
Do not force, no joint pain. Posterior pelvic tilt to protect the back. Progressive range of motion.
