## Summary
Hamstring isolation exercise on a machine. The guided movement allows precise targeting of the back of the thigh.

## Execution
- Lie face down on the bench (prone position)
- Ankles under the padded roller, just above the heels
- Grip the handles for stability
- Curl the heels toward the glutes
- Squeeze the hamstrings at the top (1-2 s)
- Lower slowly, maintaining tension

## Breathing
Exhale while curling, inhale while lowering.

## Tips
- Slow, controlled movement to maximize contraction
- Great pre-activation exercise before squats or leg press
- Do not fully release tension at the bottom

## Dosage
3-4 x 10-15 reps Rest 60-90 s (hypertrophy).
2-3 x 15-20 reps light load (endurance).

## Safety
Do not lift hips off the bench. Controlled movement, no momentum. Adjust load (knees are sensitive).
