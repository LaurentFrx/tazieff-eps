## Summary
Seated horizontal pull targeting the back (lats, mid-traps, rhomboids). Builds back thickness.

## Execution
- Sit on the bench, feet on the foot pads, knees slightly bent
- Grip the V-handle with arms extended, torso upright, chest out
- Pull the handle toward the abdomen while retracting shoulder blades
- Elbows close to the body, driving them back
- Hold the contracted position with shoulder blades squeezed
- Return in a controlled manner without rounding the back

## Breathing
Exhale while pulling toward the abdomen, inhale while releasing.

## Tips
- Do not swing the torso back and forth
- Wide grip (straight bar): emphasizes mid-traps and rhomboids
- Single-arm pull: corrects imbalances

## Dosage
3-4 x 10-12 reps Rest 60-90 s (hypertrophy).
2-3 x 15-20 reps Rest 30-60 s (endurance).

## Safety
Keep back straight, do not round. No trunk momentum. Adjust load to control full range of motion.
