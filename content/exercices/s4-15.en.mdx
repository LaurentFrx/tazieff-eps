## Summary
Develop lower body power and explosiveness through dynamic vertical jumps.

## Execution
- Lower into a squat (thighs parallel to the floor)
- Explode upward extending hips, knees and ankles
- Swing arms upward to assist the jump
- Land softly on the balls of the feet then heels, knees bent

## Breathing
Inhale during the descent, exhale on the upward drive.

## Tips
- Easier: reduce squat depth, lower jumps.
- Harder: add light dumbbells, increase jump height.
- Suggested dosage: 3-6 x 4-8 jumps, rest 60-90 s.

## Dosage
3-6 x 4-8 jumps. Rest 60-90 s. Prioritize jump quality.

## Safety
Cushioned landing: knees aligned with feet, never land with straight legs. Active core bracing throughout. Flat and stable surface.
