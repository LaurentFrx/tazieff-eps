## Summary
Develop strength and stability in the lower body (hips/knees).

## Execution
- Wide step
- knees at 90 both legs
- straight torso
- Execution: knees at 90 both legs

## Breathing
Regular (no prolonged apnea).

## Tips
- Reduce amplitude and/or use support (bench, TRX, hands).
- Add a load, increase tempo or difficulty (instability).
- 3-5 x 8-15 reps Rest 60-90 s.

## Dosage
3-5 x 8-15 reps Rest 60-90 s.

## Safety
Adapted amplitude: no joint pain. Control: active sheathing, clean movement, regular breathing.
