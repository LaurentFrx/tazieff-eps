## Summary
Strengthen the entire body and develop cardio capacity through a squat-plank-pushup-jump sequence.

## Execution
- From standing, squat down and place hands on the floor
- Kick feet back into a plank position
- Perform a full push-up (chest near the floor)
- Jump feet back toward hands and jump vertically with arms overhead

## Breathing
Exhale during the push-up and the jump. Inhale during the squat descent and return to plank.

## Tips
- Easier: remove the push-up (see s5-02) or the jump.
- Harder: increase pace, add a box jump at the top.
- Suggested dosage: 3-5 x 6-12 reps or 20-40 s work / 20-40 s rest.

## Dosage
3-5 x 6-12 reps or 20-40 s work / 20-40 s rest.

## Safety
Active core bracing in plank position (no sagging back). Full controlled push-up. Soft jump landing with knees bent.
