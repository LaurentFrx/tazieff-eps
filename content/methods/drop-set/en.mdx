## Principle

The Drop Set is an intensification technique in strength training consisting of performing a set with a given load until the inability to complete another repetition (muscular failure), then immediately reducing the load to continue the effort. This method is very effective for increasing time under tension, thus promoting muscular hypertrophy and training efficiency.

## The Drop Set in 3 Steps

1. **First set to failure** — Perform your first set with a given load until you cannot complete another repetition.
2. **Reduce the load by 20-25%** — Immediately reduce the weight, without any rest time, for the next set.
3. **Repeat to failure** — Continue with the new weight for maximum repetitions. You can do 2 to 3 "drops".

## Benefits

Push beyond muscular failure. Chain sets by lowering the load without rest for intense fatigue. Improves muscle fiber growth and condenses training volume.

## Concrete Example

**Bench Press, Max 100 kg:**

| Set | Load | Repetitions | Rest Time |
|-----|------|-------------|-----------|
| 1 | 80 kg | To failure | None |
| 2 (Drop 1) | 60 kg | To failure | None |
| 3 (Drop 2) | 40 kg | Maximum possible | 3 minutes after |
