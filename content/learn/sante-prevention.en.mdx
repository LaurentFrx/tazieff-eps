## Beware of Doping Products

⚠️ Beware of doping products sold online or in specialized stores that guarantee rapid mass gain. Most of these products are of poor quality and, above all, very dangerous for your health!

Stay clear-headed and critical in the face of these temptations. Taking these products exposes you to serious and irreversible diseases:

- Risk of infertility
- Risk of cancer
- Risk of degenerative diseases

## Your Health is Priceless

You are building it now and for the rest of your life. No aesthetic or athletic result justifies endangering your health.

Results obtained naturally, through regular training and good nutrition, are lasting and risk-free. This is the only responsible path.
