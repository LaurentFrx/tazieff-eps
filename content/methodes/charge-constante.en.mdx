## Principle

Maintain a fixed load throughout the exercise. Two options: Option 1 (high volume) 4×20 reps at 50% with a focus on muscular endurance. Option 2 (moderate intensity) 4×15 reps at 60% for transitioning to strength-endurance.

---

## Benefits

- Simple and accessible method
- Ideal for beginners
- Good base for toning and muscle definition

---

## Practical Example

Option 1 — 4 sets × 20 reps at 50% of max, 1min rest. Option 2 — 4 sets × 15 reps at 60% of max, 1min rest.

---

## Safety Tips

A foundational method accessible to all levels. Control the movement tempo carefully (1-2s up, 1-2s down).
