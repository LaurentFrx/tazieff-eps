## Principle

Combine exercises alternating agonist and antagonist (opposing) muscles. The agonist performs the movement while the antagonist opposes it. Minimal rest (15s) between the two exercises in the same set, then 2 minutes between each complete set.

---

## Benefits

- Time savings
- Better muscular activation
- Enhanced performance
- Agonist/antagonist balance

---

## Practical Example

Bicep Curl (agonist) → 15s rest → Triceps Extension (antagonist) → 2min rest. 6 sets of 10 reps at 70%. Other pairs: Bench Press / Bent-Over Row, Leg Extension / Leg Curl.

---

## Safety Tips

Master each exercise individually before combining them. Ensure you use the same relative intensity on both muscles.
