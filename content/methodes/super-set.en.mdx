## Principle

Same principle as APS but focused on rapid chaining of two exercises targeting opposing muscle groups. The goal is to increase time under tension and total training volume.

---

## Benefits

- High training volume in little time
- Active recovery of agonist during antagonist work
- Increased hormonal stimulation

---

## Practical Example

Bench press (chest) → 15s → Bent-over row (back) → 2min rest. 6 sets of 10 reps at 70%.

---

## Safety Tips

Warm up both muscle groups properly. Don't sacrifice technique for speed.
