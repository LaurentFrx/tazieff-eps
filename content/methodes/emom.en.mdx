## Principle

Perform a set number of reps at the start of each minute for 8 to 20 minutes. Rest equals the remaining time before the next minute begins. The faster you finish, the longer your recovery. Technique is the priority.

---

## Benefits

- Built-in time management
- Natural self-regulation (faster = more rest)
- Develops both cardio and muscular endurance

---

## Practical Example

EMOM 10 minutes — Each minute: 10 Air Squats + 20 Mountain Climbers + 10 Push-ups. The remaining time in the minute = rest.

---

## Safety Tips

Total duration is 8 to 20 minutes, with technique as the priority. Do not sacrifice quality for speed.
